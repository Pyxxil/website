Welcome to my website!

This website exists for several reasons, all of which I will lay out below. I hope you enjoy your stay!

To learn about Web Development

While web development has never been my forte, nor something I'm particularly interested in doing, it has its purpose. While this website offers me many opportunities that I find a lot more pressing, it would be a mistake to not actually enjoy the process, nor actually try my best to actually do it well.

To host some of my projects

This has become a lot more relevant to me now that I've spent a lot of time writing Typescript, and dabbling a little with Web Assembly (via Rust and wasm-bindgen).

A place I could put some writings

This might not be as important to anyone else as it is to me, but writing has always been my way of getting things off my mind and helping me explain things I've been thinking about.

To show off

One of the more semi-important reasons for making this website. I'm affored the opportunity to present a lot of my projects in a nicer way than with a GitHub/GitLab repository. That is to say that it allows me to provide my own flair and touch to the way they are presented, and hopefully show a bit of myself along the way.
